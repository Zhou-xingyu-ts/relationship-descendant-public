## Longer Launch Post

I packaged one of my persona-design experiments into a public OpenClaw skill:

`后裔.skill`

The hook is simple:

give it two people's chats, tone, and relationship material, and it grows a fictional same-age third persona that feels like it emerged from the way they interact.

Not "predict a real child."
More like:

"if the relationship itself could speak, who would it grow into?"

So the skill now supports:

- portrait generation
- weighted variants
- short future snapshots
- session voice conversion
- preset-style export

The framing is explicitly creative and non-diagnostic.
