## One-Line Card Copy

- Turn two people's chats, tone, and relationship cues into a fictional same-age successor persona you can actually chat with.

## Medium Description

- 后裔.skill 会把两个人的聊天、语气和关系氛围，长成一个创作型同龄第三人格，并继续扩展成变体、短场景和可长期聊天的会话人格。

## Three Bullet Sell

- Multi-modal input from chats, voice-note summaries, image descriptions, and manual notes
- Five working modes from first portrait to live session voice
- Uses the "chat your child out first" hook without pretending to predict a real future child

## Safety Line

- Creative persona synthesis only. Not a child predictor, diagnosis tool, or psychological profiler.
