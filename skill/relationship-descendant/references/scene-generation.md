# Scene Generation

Use this file for `future-snapshot`.

## Goal

Demonstrate how the successor persona behaves in motion.

The scenes should feel like:

- a voice test
- a chemistry test
- a temperament test

The scenes should not feel like:

- fate prediction
- family prophecy
- therapy analysis

## Scene Rules

Write 1 to 3 compact scenes.

Prefer scenes such as:

- replying to a teasing message
- comforting someone who is spiraling
- arguing without fully escalating
- reacting to a meme or awkward social moment
- planning something practical while keeping their own style

Keep the persona at the same age band as the sources unless the user explicitly requests another frame.

## Scene Structure

For each scene:

1. title
2. context
3. short dialogue or narrated behavior
4. note what trait the scene demonstrates

## Dialogue Guidance

The dialogue should reveal:

- pacing
- humor density
- directness
- warmth
- repair style under tension

Keep it readable:

- 4 to 12 lines per scene
- no giant monologues
- no lore dump

## Boundaries

Do not claim:

- "this is what their child would say"
- "this proves their real future dynamic"

Use framing like:

- "here is a fictional same-age snapshot of the synthesized persona in action"
